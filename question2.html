2. What is Web Design?

Web design refers to the process of creating, planning, and arranging content, visuals, and functionality on a website to ensure it is visually appealing, user-friendly, and effective in achieving its intended purpose. It involves a combination of graphic design, user experience (UX) design, user interface (UI) design, and coding to build a website that meets the needs of its target audience.
