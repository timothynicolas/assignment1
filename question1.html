1. What is World Wide Web?

The World Wide Web (WWW), commonly referred to as the Web, is a system of interconnected documents and resources, linked by hyperlinks and URLs. It is a subset of the internet that allows users to access and share information through web browsers. The Web was invented by Tim Berners-Lee in 1989 and has since become a fundamental tool for communication, education, commerce, and entertainment.
